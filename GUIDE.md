# 📖 Mess Manager — Member Guide

Welcome to the **Deep Axis Mess Manager**! This guide will help you update your daily meals, bazar costs, and understand how the system works.

🔗 **Live Site**: [https://didaralif.github.io/Deep-Axis/](https://didaralif.github.io/Deep-Axis/)

---

## 🔐 Step 1: Login

1. Open the link above in your browser
2. Select **your name** from the dropdown
3. Enter **your password** (ask Admin if you don't know it)
4. Click **Sign In**

> Your session stays active until you click **Logout** or close the browser.

---

## 🍽️ Step 2: Update Your Meals

1. Click **Meals** in the sidebar
2. Find **your row** (your name + photo)
3. For **today's date column**, type your meal count:
   - `0` = no meals, `1` = 1 meal, `2` = 2 meals, `3` = 3 meals
4. The value **auto-saves** when you click away

### ⚠️ Rules

- You can **only edit your own row** — other members' rows are disabled
- **Past dates are locked** — you cannot change yesterday's meals
- Once a cell is **verified (green)**, it cannot be edited

---

## 🛒 Step 3: Update Your Bazar Spending

1. Click **Bazar** in the sidebar
2. Scroll down to the **🛒 Bazar Cost (TK)** table
3. Find **your row** and **today's column**
4. Enter the amount you spent (in Taka)
5. If you have **miscellaneous costs** (cleaning supplies, etc.), scroll further to the **🧹 Bazar Others Cost** table and enter it there

> **Tip**: Your total spending appears in the last column of your row.

---

## 📅 Bazar Duty Slots

At the top of the **Bazar** tab, you'll see a color-coded calendar called **Bazar Duty Slots**.

- Each colored tile shows **who has bazar duty** on that day
- The **legend** at the top shows each member's color and total days
- **You cannot change the schedule** — only Admin (ALIF) can assign/reassign slots

Just check which days are yours and plan accordingly!

---

## ✅ Step 4: Verify Your Entries

Each meal and bazar cell has two small badges:

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **U** (grey) | You haven't verified yet — **click it to verify** |
| **U** (green) | You have verified ✓ |
| **A** (grey) | Admin hasn't verified yet |
| **A** (green) | Admin has verified ✓ |

### How to verify:

1. Check that your entry is correct
2. Click the **U** badge on that cell
3. The badge turns **green** ✓
4. Once **both U and A are green**, the cell locks permanently (turns green background)

> **Important**: You can only verify entries for **today and yesterday**. After that, the window closes.

> **Note**: You **cannot verify your own bazar entry** — another member must verify it for you (except Admin).

## 📊 Dashboard — Understanding Your Summary

Click **Dashboard** to see the big picture:

| Field | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Meal Rate** | Cost per meal = Total Bazar ÷ Total Meals |
| **Your Meals** | How many meals you've had this month |
| **Your Cost** | Your Meals × Meal Rate |
| **Your Deposit** | How much bazar you've done (money you put in) |
| **Balance** | Deposit − Cost (positive = others owe you) |
| **Final** | Your net balance after "Others" costs |

---

## 🏠 Rent Tab (View Only)

- Click **Rent** in the sidebar (you'll need the admin password to view)
- Shows your monthly rent, utility shares, and any extras
- You **cannot edit** rent values — only Admin can

## 🌙 Dark / Light Mode

Toggle the **Dark Mode** switch at the bottom of the sidebar to switch themes.

## ❓ Quick FAQ

**Q: I forgot to enter yesterday's meal!**
A: Past dates are locked. Ask **ALIF (Admin)** to update it for you.

**Q: My cell is green and I can't edit it!**
A: It's been fully verified and locked. Contact Admin if there's an error.

**Q: I see the wrong amount — how do I fix it?**
A: If the cell isn't locked yet, just click it and type the correct value. If it's locked, ask Admin.

**Q: Where can I see how much I owe or am owed?**
A: Check the **Dashboard** tab → look at the **Final** column in the statement table.
