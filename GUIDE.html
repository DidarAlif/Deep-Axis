📖 MessManager — Member Guide

Deep Axis Mess Management System · didaralif.github.io/Deep-Axis

1 🔐 Login

  1. Open the link above in your browser (works on phone too!)
  2. Select your name from the dropdown
  3. Enter your password (ask Admin if you don't know it)
  4. Click Sign In
💡 Your session stays active until you click Logout or close the browser.

2 🍽️ Update Your Meals

  1. Click Meals in the sidebar
  2. Find your row (your name + photo)
  3. For today's date column, type your meal count
  4. The value auto-saves when you click away
⚠️ Rules: You can only edit your own row. Past dates are locked. Green (verified) cells can't be changed.

3 🛒 Update Your Bazar Spending

  1. Click Bazar in the sidebar
  2. Scroll to the 🛒 Bazar Cost (TK) table
  3. Find your row and today's column
  4. Enter the amount you spent (in Taka)
  5. For miscellaneous costs, use the 🧹 Bazar Others table below

4 📅 Bazar Duty Slots

At the top of the Bazar tab, you'll see a color-coded calendar showing who has bazar duty on each day of the month. Check which days are yours and plan accordingly! Only Admin can modify the schedule.

5 ✅ Verify Your Entries

Each cell has two verification badges:

Badge Meaning
U (grey) You haven't verified yet — click to verify
U (green) You have verified ✓
A (grey) Admin hasn't verified yet
A (green) Admin has verified ✓

How: Click the U badge → it turns green. Once both U + A are green, the cell locks permanently.

📝 You can only verify entries for today and yesterday. You cannot verify your own bazar — another member must do it.

6 📊 Understanding the Dashboard

Field What It Means
Meal Rate Cost per meal = Total Bazar ÷ Total Meals
Your Cost Your Meals × Meal Rate
Deposit How much bazar you've done (money you put in)
Balance Deposit − Cost (positive = others owe you)
Final Your net balance after "Others" costs

☁️ Real-Time Sync

All data syncs instantly across all devices via Firebase. When you update a meal or bazar value, everyone else sees it in real-time — no refresh needed!

❓ Quick FAQ

Question Answer
I forgot yesterday's meal! Past dates are locked. Ask ALIF (Admin) to update it.
My cell is green and locked! It's fully verified. Contact Admin if there's an error.
Where do I see how much I owe? Dashboard → Final column in the statement table.
How do I switch themes? Toggle Dark Mode at the bottom of the sidebar.
